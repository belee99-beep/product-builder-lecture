Quick & Easy Pasta

A delicious and simple pasta dish that's ready in under 20 minutes. Perfect for a busy weeknight.

Why You'll Love This Quick & Easy Pasta!

  • Busy Weeknights? No Problem! This recipe is designed for speed without sacrificing flavor. Get a delicious, homemade meal on the table in less than 20 minutes, perfect after a long day.
  • Simple Ingredients, Big Flavor: You likely have most of these ingredients in your pantry already! Fresh garlic, juicy tomatoes, and fragrant oregano come together for a taste that feels gourmet.
  • Customizable Comfort: While fantastic as is, feel free to add your favorite vegetables, protein, or a sprinkle of chili flakes for an extra kick. It's your canvas!
  • Kid-Friendly & Crowd-Pleasing: A universally loved dish that even picky eaters will enjoy. It's a guaranteed hit for family dinners or casual gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (e.g., spaghetti, penne)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large pan or skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the chopped tomatoes, add the oregano, and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bring the sauce to a simmer and let it cook for about 10 minutes, or until it has thickened slightly.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to combine.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.
